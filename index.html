Introduction:-

Welcome to St Teresa's School, Berhampore

St Teresa’s School, Berhampore, is a leading Catholic English Medium Educational Institution dedicated to fostering holistic development in boys and girls. Established in 2015, the school is owned and managed by the Teresian Carmelite Sisters (CTC), an institute registered under the Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860. While established as a minority institution, we proudly welcome and admit children irrespective of caste, creed, or background, reflecting our commitment to inclusive education.
